Ingredients:
1 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup oil or melted butter
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp vanilla essence
1 egg (optional but recommended)
Steps:
Preheat oven to 180°C.
Mix sugar + egg until light (or just sugar if no egg).
Add milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix well.
Add flour + baking powder and mix until smooth batter.
Pour into a greased pan.
Bake for 25–30 minutes.
Let it cool, then slice and serve.
